About MusicSimilarityCheck

Brian McBrearty, Forensic Musicologist

Brian McBrearty is a forensic musicologist, composer, and founder of Musicologize — the parent company behind MusicSimilarityCheck. With dual degrees in Music (Theory & Composition, Jazz Studies) and Economics, he has delivered hundreds of expert similarity opinions for labels, publishers, ad agencies, and independent creators. Brian’s analyses are cited in music-copyright scholarship and law reviews, and his compositions and supervision credits include campaigns for Google, Facebook, and leading game studios. Through Mediagloss and its production library Sonitarium, Brian continues to shape sound strategy for global brands while demystifying music-copyright risk for creators.
