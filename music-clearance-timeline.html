Music Similarity Review — Timeline & Prep Checklist

Standard clearance lands in no more than two to three business days; Rush clearances are next day. Sending a high-quality mix, reference track, and good but basic metadata up front keeps the clock predictable and reliable.

1. Typical Timeline

StepStandardRush
Submission acknowledgedwithin 1 hrwithin 1 hr
Forensic review windowDay 1-2Day 0-1
Verbal feedback (if needed)Day 2Same day
Letter deliveredDay 2-3Day 1

2. Prep Checklist

  • Final or near-final mix in WAV or 320 kbps MP3
  • Reference track (if you have a “sounds-like” concern)
  • Track title & composer name(s)
  • Email  • phone  • any key deadlines

3. What Can Slow Things Down?

  • Low-bitrate demos that hide melodic detail
  • Missing reference songs mentioned in your brief
  • Last-minute mix revisions after analysis starts
